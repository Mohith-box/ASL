Behind The League

Elevating Local Sports

Abstream Sports League is a premier sports event company founded in Bengaluru, India. We are dedicated to filling the gap between casual recreational play and high-level competitive athletics.

By organizing regular Open events for all age categories on the 2nd week, and Corporate tournaments for business employees on the 4th week of every month, we provide structured schedules, official matches, and a unified rankings board.
